FAQs

Common questions about ordering, pickup/delivery, and wholesale enquiries.

How do I place an order on WhatsApp?

Add products to cart, open cart (floating cart button), fill checkout details, then tap “Place Order on WhatsApp”.

Do you have pickup and delivery?

Yes. Select Pickup or Delivery in the cart form. If you choose Delivery, address becomes required.

Can I enquire about wholesale pricing?

Yes. Use Quick Enquiry or WhatsApp Now and mention “Wholesale enquiry” with product details and quantity.

Is there an online payment gateway?

No payment gateway. Orders are placed via WhatsApp and store confirms availability and next steps.
